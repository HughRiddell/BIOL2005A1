How to use this site

Each illusion page shows the canonical version of the stimulus on the left and a control panel on the right. The controls allow you to vary specific stimulus parameters — duration, size, contrast, orientation, configuration, and so on. Make a prediction before you change a setting, then observe whether your prediction was supported. Take notes; you will need these for Part 3 of your report.

The site is best viewed on a desktop or laptop. Some illusions (Pulfrich, motion aftereffect) require sustained viewing; settle in for at least 30 seconds per observation.
